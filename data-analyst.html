Data Analyst

Learn more about this role!

A data analyst is someone who scrutinises information using data analysis tools. The meaningful results they pull from the raw data help their employers or clients make important decisions by identifying various facts and trends. Typical duties include: using advanced computerised models to extract the data needed.

    Work activities:
  • Gathering data
  • Cleaning data
  • Producing reports
  • Spotting patterns
  • Collaborating with others
  • Collecting data and setting up infrastructure

How to become a Data analyst

    Here are five steps to consider if you’re interested in pursuing a career in data science:

  • Earn a bachelor’s degree in a field with an emphasis on statistical and analytical skills, such as math or computer science
  • Learn important data analytics skills
  • Consider certification
  • Get your first entry-level data analyst job
  • Earn a master’s degree in data analytics
